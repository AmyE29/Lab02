About Me

Hi! My name is Amy.

In my past life I was in marketing. I mostly created and maintained the corporate brochures. I also was the tradeshow coordinator, which was a lot of fun, designing the booth and procuring the swag. Towards the end of my career, I started to dabble in web development, because my company was downsizing and we got rid of our webmaster, so I took on the job. This was back in the dark-ages of internet, so it wasn't very fancy, but I taught myself enough to get by, and it sparked my interest in coding.

Since then, I've been a stay-at-home mom for my 4 kids, ages 15, 13, and 10 year old twins. I kept myself busy being a room parent at the school, taking on the role of costume designer for the school musical, and being a total baseball/soccer/football mom. If life circumstances hadn't forced me back into the work place, I would have been looking to restart my career anyway. My job as "mom" was winding down, and I am needing to recreate myself as something more.

I'm really looking forward to learning all I can in these classes.
